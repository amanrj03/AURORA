# AI Product Development Engineer (Intern) --- Take-Home Assignment

**InsideIIM × Altuni AI Labs · Remote · paid · \[1-year\], with
conversion to a full-time role + PPO for strong performers.**

## About us.

InsideIIM is one of India's largest career and community platforms for
management and professional talent --- used by lakhs of students and
many of India's top employers. Altuni AI Labs (altunilabs.ai) is our AI
lab, building AI products and solutions for enterprises and consumers.
Take a look at what we've shipped → https://altunilabs.ai/ --- you'll be
building real, AI-powered features like these, end to end.

In this assignment, we want to see what you build and how you build, and
AI usage is mandatory.

## The Task

-   Build an AI Investment Research Agent: it takes a company name, does
    its research, and decides whether to invest or pass --- with the
    reasoning behind its decision.
-   That's the goal --- how you build it (what it researches, how it
    works under the hood, how it shows its results) is entirely up to
    you. We care about what you build and how you build it.

## Tech stack (this is our production stack --- please build with it)

-   React or Next.js (front end) · Node.js or Next.js (back end) ·
    LangChain.js / LangGraph.js (for the AI).
-   Within that, use any LLM provider and any other libraries / APIs /
    tools you like.
-   Use AI as much as you like while building (that's the job).
-   Deploying online (e.g. on Vercel) and sharing the link will carry
    bonus points.

## Time

-   7 days.

## What to submit

A zip file which includes your code and a README with these sections:

-   Overview --- what it does
-   How to run it --- setup and run steps (plus any keys / env needed)
-   How it works --- your approach and architecture
-   Key decisions & trade-offs --- what you chose and why, and what you
    left out
-   Example runs --- your agent's output on a few companies of your
    choice
-   What you would improve with more time
-   **BONUS points:** you are mandated to build this project using a
    choice of your AI/LLM, during which you will be chatting with the
    LLM as you build. You will get extra bonus points if you include all
    the LLM chat session transcript/logs. It will give us insight into
    your thought process and approach.

## Ground rules

-   This is a strictly solo assignment. Code sharing will be detected.
    Use AI as much as you like --- but you must be able to explain
    everything you submit.
-   If anything is ambiguous, make your own call and note it in the
    README.
